Coleslaw Salad

Description

My favorite homemade coleslaw recipe with the most delicious creamy dressing. This coleslaw is quick and easy to make. It is even easy to make in advance.

Ingredients

  • Shredded coleslaw mix
  • Good quality mayonnaise
  • Apple cider vinegar
  • Honey

Steps

  1. Make coleslaw dressing:

    2. In a small mixing bowl whisk together the mayonnaise, apple cider vinegar and honey. If needed season with a bit of salt.

  2. Pour dressing over shredded cabbage mixture:

    3. Place shredded coleslaw mix in a large bowl then pour the dressing mixture over.

  3. Toss together:

    4. Toss to evenly coat. Easy as that!
